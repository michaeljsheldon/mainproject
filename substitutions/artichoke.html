STARTERS ICONOGRAPHY INTRODUCTION


  • ¼ water

  • 5 garlic cloves

  • 1 ½ cup cashews, soaked

  • ½ cup almond milk

  • 5 tbsp nutritional yeast

  • ½ tsp salt & pepper

  • 1 (14 ounce) can artichoke hearts

  • 1 pound spinach

  • ¼ cup parmesan


Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F.

In the bowl of a food processor, add cashews, garlic, and almond milk. Puree to a cream.

Add nutritional yeast plus salt and pepper. Blend once more.

Heat a large oven safe skillet over medium heat. Once the skillet over medium heat. Once the skillet is hot, add water & garlic. Sauté for a minute or until just golden brown.

Add the artichokes and spinach to the skillet. Pour all the cheesy sauce over the artichokes and spinach. Stir to combine.

Sprinkle the top with parmesan and bake for 8-12 minutes until warmed through.
