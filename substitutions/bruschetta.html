STARTERS ICONOGRAPHY INTRODUCTION


  • 3 cups cherry tomatoes, divided

  • 3 cloves garlic, minced

  • 1 – 2 tbsp onion, minced

  • 15 leaves fresh basil

  • 2 tbsp balsamic vinegar (optional)

  • ½ tsp sea salt

  • 1/4 tsp Pepper


Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F.

Slice the tomatoes in half or in fourths. Thinly chop the onion and mince the garlic and basil. Add to a mixing bowl, and drizzle balsamic vinegar and sea salt over the top.

Transfer to a mixing bowl and add nut meal (or oat flour) and mix again until a loose dough is formed that’s firm enough to be handled. Taste and adjust seasonings as needed. I added a touch more salt.

Line a baking pan with parchment paper and bake for 30 minutes.
